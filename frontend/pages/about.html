Our Story

25 years of passion for the world's most precious spice.

✦ Est. 2000 ✦

We Exist to Share
Sri Lanka's Greatest Treasure

Ceylone Zimt was founded in 2000 by a family of spice growers from Kandy, Sri Lanka. What began as a small export operation has grown into one of the world's most respected Ceylon cinnamon brands — trusted by Michelin-starred chefs, wellness practitioners, and home cooks across 48 countries.

Our mission has never changed: to bring the world's most extraordinary cinnamon — true Cinnamomum verum — directly from the hands of master peelers to your kitchen.

✦ Our Commitment ✦

Sustainability at Our Core

🌳 Reforestation

For every order shipped, we plant one cinnamon tree in partnership with local farmers. Over 50,000 trees planted to date.

♻️ Eco Packaging

All packaging is 100% biodegradable, made from recycled materials. Zero single-use plastic in our supply chain.

💡 Solar Power

Our processing facility runs 100% on solar energy, dramatically reducing our carbon footprint.

🎓 Community

We fund education for farmers' children through the Ceylon Spice Foundation, supporting 200+ students annually.
